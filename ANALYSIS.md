## 8. Methodology Notes

### Data Generation
- **PRNG:** mulberry32 with seed=42 (deterministic, reproducible)
- **Volume:** 11,000 deals across 29-month period
- **Distribution:** Random product, client, source, rep assignment
- **Pricing:** ±10% variance on base price, ±5% on cost
- **CAC:** Fixed per-channel values (not deal-specific)

### Metrics Definitions
- **Realized Revenue:** Gross revenue after quantity and price variance
- **Net Profit:** Revenue - Product Cost - CAC - Operating Expense (3% of revenue)
- **ROI:** Net Profit / Total CAC Spend
- **Margin:** Net Profit / Realized Revenue × 100

### Limitations
- Simulated data — actual market dynamics not modeled
- No seasonality weighting in deal generation
- Fixed CAC per channel (no volume discount modeling)
- No pipeline/forecast data — retrospective analysis only
